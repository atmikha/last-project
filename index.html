STICK BY THESE RULES

Your main goal is to pop maximum Bubbles before you run out of time. And your mouse is your bubble shooter. Aim right and click to shoot.

Remember to aim at the special bubbles that may pop up once in awhile. If you pop them right, you will score bonus time.
